WHAT DO YOU CHOOSE TO DO?

Remember your resolve and close your door

A World Health Organization Report has classified bacon and sausage as carcinogenic to humans.

It isn’t possible to live a perfect life, and if you try to hold yourself to a perfect vegan life, you will eventually fail. Instead, remember the reasons why you are interested in eating this way, and remember that every time you chose to exclude animal products from your diet, you are making a positive difference.
