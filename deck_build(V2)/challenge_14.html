Remind yourself of your reasons for your veganism

Learning to deal with people who are made uncomfortable by your decision to live differently from them is one of the most difficult aspects of being vegan. People who try to intimidate or scare vegans into conforming to their omnivore way of life can be a huge deterrent for someone who is still new to the diet. The best way to combat this negativity is to arm yourself with facts.
You will, more likely than not, encounter someone who does not want to accept your lifestyle or diet as a vegan. It it still culturally normal and acceptable to eat meat, so it may appear to be socially extreme to change your lifestyle, habits, and choices – but that doesn’t mean that your choices are wrong, and these awkward confrontations can be an opportunity to educate your friends.
