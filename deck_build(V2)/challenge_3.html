Come out as vegan to your dad

The most important thing to keep in mind is that there is no such thing as a perfect vegan. If you make a mistake, or chose to “break vegan” for any number of reasons, it doesn’t change the larger positive impact that your diet has created.
Don’t worry! Let them know how to veganize all of the family favorites. They’ll taste delicious, and your family will realize that the dish, and you, are still the same as before -just vegan!

It’s best to let your relatives know ahead of time that you’re vegan. You can offer to prepare a dish to share or ask to help in the kitchen so that you’ll have a main course to enjoy.
