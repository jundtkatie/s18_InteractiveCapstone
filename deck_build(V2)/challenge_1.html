Remember that being vegan is about reducing suffering – it’s not about being perfect. So if you are obsessing over micro-ingredients, try and remember that helping animals, your health, and the environment can be as simple as choosing a bean burrito over a steak one, or tofu scramble instead of eggs.

Pancakes, waffles and muffins are easy to make, and the internet is filled with delicious vegan recipes for them. But if sweet or starchy breakfasts aren’t for you, silken tofu is a tasty egg alternative in omelets, scrambles, and Quiche.
