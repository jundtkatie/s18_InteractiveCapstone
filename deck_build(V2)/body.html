WHAT DOES IT MEAN TO BE VEGAN?

Veganism
NOUN
A person who does not eat or use animal products: I'm a vegan.

ADJECTIVE
Using or containing no animal products: A vegan diet.
represents a philosophy and way of living which seeks to exclude—as far as is possible and practicable—all forms of exploitation of, and cruelty to, other animals for food, clothing or any other purpose; and promotes the development and use of animal-free alternatives for the benefit of humans, other animals and the environment.
